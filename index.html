About

Learn More About Me

  • Origins

    Growing up in Indiana, I found my love for the arts from my grandparents and family who have supported me in everything I did. In my free time, I would always be drawing or performing for an audience.

  • High School

    In high school, I was a member of the arts community; graduating with top honors in Art, Theater and Band. I was also a part of the men’s swimming & diving team. With support from my Grandfather, I earned the rank of Eagle Scout and became an Assistant Scout Master for our local Boy Scout Troop.

  • College

    While studying at Indiana University, I was a Resident Leader of the Art Housing and managed our personal art studio. I worked with my residents to coordinate and host gallery nights for the public. During my sophomore year, I participated in the Disney College Program and became a campus representative leader.

  • Post-Graduation

    I started my career as a Professional Intern at Disney and decided that Disney is where I wanted to launch my career. Over the years, I have had experience working with domestic and international clients expanding on my knowledge with every new position and project.

  • Today

    I am a Creative Designer for Talent Acquisition Marketing for The Walt Disney Company creating materials such as print, digital, photography and video. I am a mentor for interns at work and local students involved in AIGA Orlando.

DISNEY CASTING SCOUT

Internal Employee Referral Program

Disney Casting Scout is an internal referral program that gives Cast Members the opportunity to refer exceptional talent to join the Disney family. With the launch of a new advocacy strategy, I wanted to refresh the branding to solve challenges experienced with the old brand and executions. Utilizing the original Mickey artwork, I wanted to create a dynamic layout that would elevate the overall visual of the program and feature better hierarchy for communication.

  • Date: January 2019
  • Client: Disney Casting Services
  • Category: Brand, Print, Digital and Promotions

DISNEY DREAM JOB HIRING EVENT

Internal/External Job Fair Mini Campaign

Over the course of the year, our recruitment strategy has been to feature seasonal Job Fair mini campaigns to create ongoing interest within the job market. We kicked off the year with the Disney Dream Job Hiring Event Campaign which ran for both Disneyland® and Walt Disney World® for two months. Each mini campaign requires over 100 marketing tactics from digital to large format printing and is typically turned within 2 to 3 weeks.

  • Date: January 2019
  • Client: Disney Casting Services
  • Category: Brand, Print, and Digital

DISNEY PROFESSIONAL INTERNSHIPS

TWDC Enterprise Internship

The Disney Professional Internship brand has evolved a few times since it’s last debut in 2012. With the current expansions and reorganizations of The Walt Disney Company, I proposed some changes to better align with our Disney Careers recruitment and add flexibility for future branding updates. This new creative direction required a photoshoot that involved contracts and art direction that I facilitated remotely from across the country.

  • Date: October 2018
  • Client: Enterprise Campus – Disney Professional Internships
  • Category: Brand, Photography, Print and Social Media

DISNEY ALUMNI ASSOCIATION

Internship Alumni Program

The Disney Alumni Association is a group that was developed to celebrate those who completed their Disney College Program. DAA is a strategy used to generate communication and referrals for the Disney College Program. In 2018, the association integrated the alumni participants of the Disney International Programs. As a way to be inclusive of both domestic and international programs, I decided to refresh this brand by updating the color pallet and work with my communication partners to redirect messaging and improve platforms for the new audiences.

  • Date: August 2018
  • Client: Disney Campus Programs, Disney Alumni Association
  • Category: Brand, Web Strategy, Digital and Social communication

DISNEY HOSPITALITY LEADERSHIP PROGRAM

Elite Hospitality Internship Program at Disney Resorts

The Disney Hospitality Leadership Program was created to recruit the top talent at elite international hospitality schools to enhance the guest service at Disney resorts. Requested by executive leadership, this new program comprised of a full-service roadshow: Print materials, booth set-up and executive recruitment presentations. The Disney Hospitality program has expanded to over 3 Disney Parks, including our latest park, Disney Shanghai Resort.

  • Date: October 2017
  • Client: Disney International Programs, Disneyland Paris, Shanghai Disney Resort
  • Category: Brand, Photography, Print, Digital and Promotions

DISNEY AUDITIONS

Disney International Tours Campaign

Disney Parks Live Entertainment hosts auditions for various roles that are contracted for 6-8 months overseas at our Disney Theme Parks & Resorts. The challenge was creating a new campaign that would create visual interest, tell a story and work as a cohesive campaign. Inspired by participant stories, this campaign features the iconic Disney castles from the international parks and highlights major landmarks from each city’s respective skyline.

  • Date: September 2016
  • Client: Disney Parks Live Entertianment
  • Category: Brand, Print, Digital and Web Solutions

DISNEY CRUISE LINE

Entertainment Asset Collection & Lifeguard Recruitment Sizzle Video

The purpose of this project was to refresh photo and video assets used in various recruitment materials such as presentations, social posts and print collateral. This production featured entertainment and lifeguard shipboard positions. I worked with DCL producers and shipboard teams to coordinate casting of crew and extras for a port day shoot. I was art director for both of the photo and video shoots happening simultaneously within tight operational timeframes.

  • Date: March 2017
  • Client: Disney Cruise Line Recruitment
  • Category: Brand, Photography and Video Production

BEHIND THE NAME TAG SERIES

Video Series for Advance Job Descriptions

Behind the Name Tag is a series of short videos used to demystify the employment opportunities at Disney. Following key learnings about video optimization, I came up with a storytelling structure that will allow our team to brand new content and revitalize older content. The inspiration for this series is to show Disney Cast Members in their roles and seeing who is truly behind the name tag. I worked with major production vendors to coordinate shoots and worked with our copy writers to develop scripts and storyboards.

  • Date: May 2017
  • Client: Disney Casting Services
  • Category: Brand and Video Production

DISNEY SAFETY

Photo and Video Series for Recruitment

Disney Safety is a team comprised of Ergonomists, Industrial Hygienists and Occupational Safety professionals who are in charge of making sure that guest and cast experiences and environments are safe on a daily basis. This photo collection and video series was used to showcase unique experiences that only Disney can provide and to highlight employment opportunities for this team.

  • Date: April 2015
  • Client: Disney Professional Recruitment
  • Category: Brand, Photography and Video Production
